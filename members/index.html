Professor
박재현 (Jaehyun Park)
Assistant Professor

Jaehyun Park received his Ph.D. degree from the Graduate School of Information Security at Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), Daejeon, Korea, in 2019. He worked at the Korea Financial Telecommunications and Clearings Institute (KFTC) in the Financial Authentication Center and Card Infrastructure Systems Dept. until 2026. Currently, he is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Engineering at Hanbat National University. His research interests include biometrics, user authentication, privacy and optimization of video streaming, IoRT security and Privacy, and network security.
